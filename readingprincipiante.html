Actividad 1

I love my family

I live in a big house with all my family. There are seven of us, including my grandfather, father, mother, and my younger siblings. I have two younger brothers and an older sister.

My grandfather came to this country as an immigrant. He found a job on a ranch, working with horses. He then met my grandmother and they soon married. They had only one child; she is my mother.

Everyone gets along well with each other. We make sure that we do our chores before our father reminds us. We also love to gather around grandfather when he tells one of his stories about growing up in the old country
